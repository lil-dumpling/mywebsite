Use Against Japan

By Christopher Abacan


On August 6th, 1945, a Boeing B-29 Superfortress called Enola Gay with Little Boy in its belly passed over the city of Kokura, Japan. the sky was cloudy, so the crew chose to go to their secondary target, Hiroshima. Three days later, Fat man was dropped over Nagasaki. The death toll was ~185,000 - 226,000.
